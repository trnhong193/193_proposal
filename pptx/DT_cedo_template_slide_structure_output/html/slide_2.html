Project Requirement Statement

  • Project: AI-Powered Video Analytics for Safety Monitoring and Compliance
  • Project Owner: Cedo Vietnam
  • Work Scope: Cloud-based AI system to monitor workplace safety in real-time, transforming existing CCTV infrastructure into a proactive safety monitoring solution. The system will detect PPE compliance and unsafe behaviors to prevent safety incidents.
  • Project Duration: Q1/2026 (Site survey scheduled for 1st week of January 2026)
  • Camera Number: 17 cameras - Workshop VN1: 9 cameras (Verkada CD41 and CD41-E) - Workshop VN2: 8 cameras (Verkada CD41 and CD41-E)
  • Number of AI Module per Camera: 2-3 modules per camera (average)
  • AI Modules: 1. Helmet Detection 2. Hi-vis vest detection 3. Safety shoes detection 4. Safety Gloves Detection 5. Anti Collision
