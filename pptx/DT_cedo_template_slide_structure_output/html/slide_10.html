Safety shoes detection

Purpose:

Detects workers without safety shoes before entering the site and/or during working at the site. Verifies proper footwear usage. Safety shoes protect against hazards like falling objects or sharp materials.

Alert Logic:

Alert will be sent out immediately to site supervisors when workers without safety shoes are detected.

Preconditions:

Camera must maintain a suitable distance for clear observation of workers, typically between 5 to 10 meters.

Data Requirements:

Not applicable (standard module)
