Dashboard Visualizations

  • Event Analysis: Charts and graphs showing detection frequencies for each module, safety compliance rates, and incident trends.
  • Alert Timelines: Chronological view of all safety incidents and alerts, with filtering by module type, location, and time period.
  • Evidence Snapshots: Image and video clips of detected events, including timestamp and location information.
  • Custom KPIs: Standard KPIs include:
  • PPE compliance rate (helmet, vest, shoes, gloves)
  • Unsafe behavior incidents (human-machine proximity violations)
  • Alert frequency by module and location
  • Safety trend analysis
  • Multi-Dashboard: Web-based dashboard accessible from multiple locations, with role-based access control.
